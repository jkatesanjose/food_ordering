Discover 4J's Food Services story

4J's Food Services is a family business owned by Maricel San Jose. It was established in 2022. Maricel San Jose has a strong passion for cooking, so she and her family decided to start a small business. They don't have a physical store or restaurant for customers to dine in, but their home serves as their store, where they prepare all customer orders. All the dishes they cook are exclusively delivered by them. The name "4J's" comes from her four children, whose names all start with the letter J. Everything offered by 4J's Food Services is available for all four meals (breakfast, lunch, snack, and dinner). They have a party tray that's perfect for all occasions and bento boxes that are a great deal because they include a main dish and a side dish.
